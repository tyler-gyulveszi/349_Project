About Our Owner!

Hows it going! My name is Tyler Gyulveszi, and im the owner of Chef.IT! Here at Chef.IT we are dedicated to bringing you most wonderful recipes, flavors, and helpfulness that we can offer. We offer weekly updates to our website where we publish the newest meals we can, and give our step by step directions on how we approached our concoctions. We also tell you the best places in our local area to find them for our local chefs! Our mission is to make everyone feel the beauty of cooking!

I know what you're thinking, why should we follow directions from some novice who enjoys cooking as a passtime? Well, cooking has meant a lot to me for quite some time. My first memories of me in a kitchen were when I was 4 or 5 years old cooking with my mom, she just loved having an extra pair of hands. As I got older I started to challenge myself more and more to make more delicious and challenging foods, constantly making family and friends try my cooking. As time went on I realized that I truly loved being in the kitchen, it was a theraputic safe place where I could stowe away for hours on end. I wanted everyone to be able to experience the same joy I feel, and I realized everyone doesn't have the luxuries of having someone to show them how to cook. I decided to use my programming skills to give back to those who are looking to experience a good, home cooked meal. I hope I do an okay job!
